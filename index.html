More Information

Lora is also a passionate classical guitar teacher. All ages and abilities welcome, ranging from complete beginners to more experienced players looking to expand their skills on the instrument. Lora teaches on the North Shore, Auckland (a ten-minute bus from Albany), which is easily accessible by public transport.
