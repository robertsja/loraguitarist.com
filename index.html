About Me

Hello! I'm Alex Morgan, a musician based in Portland, Oregon. My journey with music began at the age of 6 when I first touched a piano, and it's been my passion ever since.

After studying music composition at Berklee College of Music, I've spent the last decade performing across the country and releasing three studio albums. My sound is a unique blend of folk storytelling, jazz improvisation, and electronic textures.

When I'm not performing or recording, I teach music theory and composition at the local community college and volunteer with music therapy programs for at-risk youth.
